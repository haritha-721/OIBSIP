Biography


Elon Reeve Musk (born June 28, 1971) is the founder of Paypal, SpaceX, Tesla, The Boring Company, Solar City, and many more. Elon Musk is a visionary entrepreneur and investor who has revolutionized the fields of space exploration, electric vehicles, artificial intelligence and social media. He is the founder, CEO and chief engineer of SpaceX, which aims to make humans a multiplanetary species; the CEO and product architect of Tesla, which produces sustainable energy solutions and innovative cars; the owner and CEO of Twitter, which connects millions of people around the world; the founder of the Boring Company, which digs tunnels for fast transportation; the co-founder of Neuralink, which develops brain-computer interfaces; and the co-founder of OpenAI, which researches artificial general intelligence. He is also the president of the Musk Foundation, which supports renewable energy, human space exploration, pediatric research and science education. He is the second-wealthiest person in the world as of May 202312, with an estimated net worth of around $167 billion12. He was born in South Africa in 1971 and became a citizen of Canada and later the United States. He has 10 children from his previous marriages and relationships1. He is widely regarded as one of the most influential and innovative people of our time.
